Chocolate

Modern Design

Chocolate is a modern and intuitive software, which will allow you to enjoy your favorite cinema with ease.

Full support

Chocolate allows you to enjoy all your favorite movies, series, books, TV channels, home videos and even your favorite retro games.

Easy-to-use settings

Chocolate is configurable to your liking, you can choose your language, create your libraries, choose who can access which libraries, and much more!
